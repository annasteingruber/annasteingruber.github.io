Germany

Introduction

Germany is a key member of the continent's economic and political rganizations.

European power struggles immersed Germany in two world wars in the 20th century and left the country occupied by the Allied powers of the US, UK, France, and the Soviet Union in 1945.

People And Society

Population

The country has an estimated population of 84,316,622 as of 2022.

Germany is the 18th most populated country in the world.

Ethnic Groups

Top groups by nationality include German, Turkish, Polish, and Syrian.

Government

Citizenship

Citizenship by parentage comes when at least one parent must be a German citizen or a resident alien who has lived in Germany at least 8 years.

Branches of Government

Germany is a federal parliamentary republic. It has a civil law system.

Environment

Climate

The climate is temperate and marine, with cool, cloudy, wet winters and summers.
